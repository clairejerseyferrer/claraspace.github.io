WHO'S CLARA?


Clara Orpheus is a pen name used by Claire Jersey Ferrer, the real name of the author. On Facebook, Clara posts writer updates, short stories (mostly comedic), and poems.

On Wattpad, she goes by the name Riellaine Caldwell, where she focuses on writing novels that feature character development, tragedies, and heartbreaks. She believes in optimism despite tough situations and that everything has a reason to mold people into their better selves.

Claire writes for fun and as an outlet for her emotions during past hardships.
